What is VPN

A VPN, or Virtual Private Network, is an online security tool that encrypts your internet connection. With a VPN, your data is shielded from online snoopers, providing privacy and security while browsing the internet. Use a VPN to access websites securely and anonymously, as well as to protect your personal information from online threats.
